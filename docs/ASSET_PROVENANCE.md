## Required evidence for new assets

A pull request adding or replacing an asset must state:

- creator or source;
- date obtained or created;
- applicable license or written permission;
- whether it contains personal, customer, station, device, or project information;
- sanitization steps; and
- whether commercial/OEM redistribution rights are available.

Unclear-provenance assets must not be included in a commercial deliverable until their rights are resolved.
